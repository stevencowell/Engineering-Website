Year 9 Engineering Program

Scope & sequence with direct links to each toolkit focus on the website

This guide mirrors the website flow—Term 1 Toolkit 1 through Term 4 Toolkit 4—so you can walk the program “one step at a time”. Each link jumps straight to the matching toolkit section on the term pages.

Term 1 – Structures

Build WHS foundations, investigate materials, analyse structural behaviour and document tower/beam testing.

Toolkit 1

WHS & Risk Control

OnGuard certifications, hierarchy of control, PPE routines for hand and power tools, DRABC first response, and SWMS drafting before practical work.

  • Complete safety induction, PPE checks and SWMS drafts for tower/beam tasks
  • Workshop signage activity, DRABC overview and first-aid practice
  • Safe setup and use of core hand tools, drills and glue guns
Toolkit 2

Materials & Corrosion

Classify engineering materials, run corrosion and damp-proof experiments, and gather evidence for reports.

  • Saltwater soak tests on mild steel, gal steel, plastic and concrete
  • Damp-proof course brick stack experiment with membrane control
  • Materials brief on properties, appearance, durability and sustainability
Toolkit 3

Structural Concepts

Analyse loads, SI units, struts vs ties and triangulation before scaling your tower builds.

  • Force types, reactions, point vs distributed loading
  • Strength-to-weight and slenderness for compression vs tension members
  • Bridge basics, truss forms and PBS simulator practice
Toolkit 4

Build, Test & Evaluate

Construct and destructively test the water tower and concrete beam, recording data and evaluations for the folio.

  • Planned build sequence, quality checks and SWMS sign-off
  • Load testing to first crack and failure with video/photo evidence
  • Folio sections on data tables, graphs, evaluation and improvements

Term 2 – Mechanisms & Fluid Power

Move from fluid power and lever linkages to compound gear trains and reliable dispensing, with CAD/Onshape support.

Toolkit 1

Hydraulic Digger

Fluid power, syringe sizing, lever geometry; build and test a tabletop digger.

  • Pascal’s principle, force/area ratios, MA/VR predicted vs measured
  • Build, plumb, de‑air; measure effort, travel, load; basic efficiency notes
  • SWMS for water/pressure hazards; photos + data for folio
Toolkit 2

Geared Lolly Dispenser

Compound gears, optional cam/ratchet, reliability and user‑centred design.

  • Design gear trains; predict vs measure turns‑per‑candy
  • Prototype gate/drum; add ratchet/anti‑backdrive if needed
  • Reliability/jam log (e.g., 20+ cycles); hygiene/safety/guarding considerations
Toolkit 3

Mechanism Experiments

MA/VR tests, cams/linkages, friction/reliability investigations.

  • Simple gear pair/lever/pulley/cam tests; collect ratios/efficiency data
  • Motion mapping of inputs/outputs; note friction effects
  • Short data tables and reflections for folio
Toolkit 4

CAD & Onshape (Support)

Layouts/assemblies/drawings to support digger/dispenser builds.

  • Sketch/assemble key parts; mates/configs as needed
  • Create drawings/screenshots for folio with dimensions and notes
  • Export and organise evidence to integrate with practical builds

Term 3 – Project Realisation

Dedicated workshop time to build, test and refine the Hydraulic Digger and Geared Lolly Dispenser.

Toolkit 1

Hydraulic Digger Build

Finalise construction and conduct performance testing for the formative project.

  • Assemble frame and arms; mount syringes securely
  • Conduct lifting tests; record input force vs load lifted
  • Evaluate control smoothness and hydraulic efficiency
Toolkit 2

Lolly Dispenser Build

Construct the summative project with compound gears and reliable dispensing.

  • Fabricate frame and mount gear train with correct spacing
  • Install dispensing drum, gate, and optional cam/ratchet
  • Test for jams and consistency; adjust tolerances
Toolkit 3

Mechanism Experiments

Support your builds with targeted experiments on simple machines.

  • Verify gear ratios and lever MA calculations with rig tests
  • Investigate friction losses and efficiency improvements
  • Document experimental data for the folio
Toolkit 4

Folio & CAD

Compile all design, construction, and testing evidence into the final assessment folio.

  • Finalise CAD drawings and assembly views
  • Collate testing data, graphs, and reliability logs
  • Write evaluations and sustainability reflections

Term 4 – Mechanisms & Manufacture

Capstone term focusing on digital manufacture, advanced CAD and showcase preparation for the geared dispenser.

Toolkit 1

Timber Materials

Material selection, fabrication WHS and sustainable sourcing for mechanism casings.

  • Compare timber species and sheet goods for strength, machinability and finish quality
  • Plan cut lists, jigs and clamping with PPE for sawing, drilling and sanding
  • Apply surface prep/finishes and manage waste, offcuts and dust safely
Toolkit 2

Onshape & CAD Mastery

Advanced modelling workflows, configurations and production-ready drawings.

  • Build assemblies with mates, variables/configurations and clear naming/BOMs
  • Create multi-sheet drawings with section/detail views, tolerances and title blocks
  • Export DXF/DWG for laser/CNC and capture part callouts for manufacture
Toolkit 3

CAMs, Gears & Manufacture

Laser/CNC prep, CAM toolpaths and assembly of final dispenser components.

  • Set kerf/allowances for laser/CNC files; verify fits for gears, cams and shafts
  • Generate and simulate toolpaths; organise cut files and safe machine setups
  • Assemble mechanisms with fasteners/adhesives, guards and alignment checks
Toolkit 4

Testing, Folios & Showcase

System testing, data capture, sustainability reporting and expo-ready presentation.

  • Test dispense counts, jams, torque/effort and durability; log data with photos/video
  • Iterate fixes and document changes; update folio with graphs, tables and QA checks
  • Prepare sustainability and safety summaries plus final boards/demo for showcase
