What The Old Folios Showed

Rough note style: wiring issue, pump does not turn off, fixed.

This is useful evidence, but it is too thin for a folio because it does not explain the cause, test, fix or result.

Improved folio style: During testing, the pump stayed on after the moisture sensor changed state. We traced the fault to the relay/power wiring, insulated the exposed positive wire, then retested the system. The final test showed the pump switched off when the soil reading was above the threshold.

That is the job: turn rough workshop notes into clear engineering evidence. No waffle, no mystery meat.
