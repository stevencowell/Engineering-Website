Design Brief and Success Criteria

Design, build, tune, and test a tabletop hydraulic digger. Your folio needs to show the engineering decisions you made, the evidence you collected, and how your test results prove how well the hydraulic and linkage systems performed.

Design Brief

  • Create a hydraulic digger with boom, dipper and bucket movement.
  • Use syringes, tubing and lever linkages to transfer force and movement.
  • Plan the linkage geometry, syringe sizing, build sequence and test method before cutting parts.

Success Criteria

  • Digger has three working circuits and moves smoothly without leaks or binding.
  • Folio includes sketches, photos, build log, test data, calculations and evaluation.
  • Evaluation explains reach, lift, movement, losses and specific improvements.

Evidence To Collect

  • Sketches, pivot layout and syringe mount photos.
  • Syringe sizes, piston areas, hose routing and de-airing evidence.
  • Reach, lift, input/output travel, MA/VR calculations and tuning evidence.

Key Vocabulary

  • Pressure, force, area, circuit, piston, fulcrum, load, effort, travel.
  • Pascal's principle, mechanical advantage, velocity ratio, efficiency, friction, air lock.
