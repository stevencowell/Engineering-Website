Year 9 Industrial Technology – Engineering

Task 2 – Projects & Reports 2

Marking Criteria

Total: 100 marks (scaled to 40%)

Task 2 Marking Criteria
Criterion High (A-range) Sound (B–C range) Basic (D–E range)
1. WHS & Workshop Conduct (IND5-1, IND5-6)
20 marks		 Maintains high-level WHS practice independently; demonstrates teamwork, responsibility, and consistent tool competence. Mostly safe; occasional reminders needed; engages with teamwork when prompted. Frequent reminders required; unsafe or inconsistent workshop conduct.
2. Engineering Project Construction (IND5-2, IND5-3, IND5-4, IND5-7)
30 marks		 Highly accurate, well-finished project; effective material choice; strong demonstration of engineering concepts (e.g. tension/compression, buoyancy, mechanisms, or CAD-based fabrication). Project is structurally sound with minor inaccuracies; reasonable material choice; competent workmanship. Project poorly constructed, unstable, unfinished, or technically incorrect.
3. Engineering Folio (Term 2 concepts) (IND5-5, IND5-8, IND5-9, IND5-10)
35 marks		 Folio is comprehensive and polished; includes design iterations, CAD/Onshape screenshots, calculations or tests, WHS notes, evaluation, and links to emerging technologies; communicates ideas clearly. Folio mostly complete with required elements; communication generally clear; some CAD or testing evidence present. Folio missing several required elements; weak communication; limited CAD or testing evidence.
4. Evaluation & Engineering Reasoning (IND5-8, IND5-10)
15 marks		 Insightful reasoning; strong use of engineering terminology; deep reflection on performance, sustainability, energy efficiency, or stability; clear proposed improvements. Basic reasoning; some valid observations; improvements identified in general terms. Minimal evaluation; lacks engineering insight; improvements unclear or absent.

These criteria should be used in conjunction with the official Task 2 Assessment Notification.
