Testing & Observation Checklist

Safety Prep

  • SWMS reviewed and controls in place for tower/beam testing.
  • PPE on: eye protection, enclosed shoes, apron/long sleeves as required; hair and cords secured.
  • Work area clear; clamps/fixtures set; fall zone clear; first aid kit accessible; supervisor aware.
  • Load path safe: bucket/plate centred, no hands under load, clear stop protocol if instability occurs.

Measurement & Data

  • Record tower/beam mass before testing.
  • Capture load increments and total load at failure (note timing of first deflection).
  • Note deflection/lean at each major increment (estimate or measure at marked reference points).
  • Identify first failure point: member buckling, joint shear/peel, concrete crack, tension break.

Evidence Capture

  • Photos: build stages, joints, final assembly, test setup, failure close-up.
  • Video: side and front angles during loading, showing deflection and failure.
  • Costing/scale sheet updated (tower) and material list saved.
  • Reflection drafted: what failed first, why, and one change to improve stiffness/weight/corrosion protection.
