Welcome to the Stage 5 Engineering Hub

This page is your central access point for all learning materials for the Stage 5 Engineering course (Year 9 & 10). The course covers four key focus areas: Structures, Mechanisms, Control Systems, and Engineering Specialisation.

Navigate through the modules below to access specific unit content, including:

  • Comprehensive theory notes and explanations
  • Hands-on design tasks and practical projects
  • Assessment requirements and folio checklists
  • Safety guidelines and risk management resources
  • Links to Google Classroom activities

Getting Started: Begin with Term 1 to build foundational knowledge in structures, materials, and workplace health and safety. Each term builds upon previous learning, so we recommend following the sequence.
