Digger Dynamics

Drive the digger, dig soil from the mound, carry it carefully, lift the bucket over the truck tailgate, angle the arm properly and place the load into the tray. The Engineering view shows pivots, lever arms and hydraulic force.

Objective

Dig and deliver 480 kg of soil into the truck tray by lifting over the tailgate and angling the arm correctly before time runs out.

Tip: tuck the arm when lifting. Reach is handy, but it costs force.

Student missions

  • Dig a clean scoop from the mound.
  • Lift over the tailgate, angle the arm and dump one load into the truck.
  • Turn on Engineering view and identify three pivots.
  • Deliver a load while force stays under 180 N.
  • Save a screenshot for folio evidence.

Classroom evidence

Use the screenshot button after a successful scoop/dump. Students can paste it into their folio and explain the lever advantage.

Prompt: Which arm position gave the best lifting advantage, and why?
