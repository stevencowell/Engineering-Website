Year 9 Industrial Technology – Engineering

Task 1 – Projects & Reports 1

Marking Criteria

Total: 100 marks (scaled to 30%)

Task 1 Marking Criteria
Criterion High (A-range) Sound (B–C range) Basic (D–E range)
1. WHS & Risk Management (IND5-1)
20 marks		 Consistently demonstrates exemplary WHS practice; independently identifies and controls risks; correct tool use at all times; clear WHS documentation. Usually safe and responsible; minor prompting required; basic WHS documentation completed. Frequent unsafe behaviour or reliance on teacher intervention; WHS documentation incomplete or inaccurate.
2. Practical Project Quality (IND5-2, IND5-3, IND5-4, IND5-7)
30 marks		 Project is accurate, well-constructed, and functions as intended; excellent material selection and tool use; strong evidence of skill development. Project is mostly accurate with minor issues; acceptable material selection; competent tool use. Project incomplete or inaccurate; weak craftsmanship and/or poor material selection.
3. Engineering Folio / Report (IND5-5, IND5-8, IND5-9, IND5-10)
30 marks		 Folio is detailed, logical, and well-presented; includes planning, sketches, WHS notes, photos, material justification, and evaluation; uses correct engineering terminology. Folio includes most required elements; generally clear and organised; some engineering terminology used. Folio is incomplete, missing major components, unclear, or poorly organised; minimal engineering language.
4. Evaluation (PMI + testing reflection) (IND5-8, IND5-10)
20 marks		 Thoughtful evaluation; clear testing data; insightful strengths and weaknesses; meaningful improvements identified. Basic evaluation with some reflection; limited discussion of improvements. Minimal or superficial reflection; lacks testing evidence; improvements unclear.

These criteria should be used in conjunction with the official Task 1 Assessment Notification.
